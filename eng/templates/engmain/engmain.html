Azlex Legal Bureau is one of leading national law firm in the legal market of Uzbekistan and the Central Asia. Our office provides a wide range and comprehensive legal support to Uzbek and foreign companies with wide range legal issues, related to business activities on the territory of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

The advocate bureau «Azlex Business Consulting Group» was founded in 2009. Director of Bureau is experienced, highly qualified advocate, specialist in the Uzbek and International law – Azimov Ilkhom Ulugbekovich.

Our law firm is the main consultant for the business leaders in different market segments of the economy in general, representatives of small and medium-sized business, foreign investors that interested in entry to the Uzbek market and active realization of the projects on the territory of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

Lawyers of our office are highly-qualified legal consultants, have a master and doctoral degrees, with practical experience in Russia, USA, United Kingdom. Also, our lawyers worked at the Uzbek governmental and law enforcement bodies, are fluent in foreign languages, have expert knowledge in the sphere of the national and international law.

The lawyers of Bureau have an experience of work with international law firms, experience in structuring international investment projects, incorporation of joint ventures, purchasing of assets, drafting international contracts, reducing legal investment risks, tax planning, international dispute resolution.

Among the clients of the office are international companies, banks, foreign representations, joint stock companies, public corporations, projects financed by the IFIs.
